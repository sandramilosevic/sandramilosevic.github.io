Skincare Serum Promo Video

Overview

During my internship at Can, our team worked on a group project focused on creating a promotional video for a skincare product. The project simulated a real-world marketing brief, to concept, design, and produce an advertisement for a serum inspired by the minimalist aesthetic of The Ordinary.

Goal

The goal was to create a short promotional video that conveys purity, simplicity, and product efficacy through a clean and minimal visual language. The video needed to feel authentic and aligned with the skincare industry's refined visual standards.

Process

We started by analyzing The Ordinary’s brand identity: neutral tones, minimalist composition, and a focus on texture and detail.
Based on this research, we developed a concept emphasizing natural beauty and transparency, using close-up product shots and smooth transitions to create a calm, refined atmosphere.
I was responsible for video editing and assisted during the shoot with lighting setup and shot coordination.

Full Scenario (PDF)

Outcome

The final 25-second video presents the serum application process through a clean, minimalist visual language.
Soft natural lighting, macro shots, and slow pacing emphasize texture, transparency, and purity, echoing The Ordinary’s recognizable aesthetic.

Reflection

Working on this project as part of a creative team helped me understand the importance of collaboration and clear communication throughout the production process.
I learned how to adapt my work to complement others' roles, from filming to editing, and how teamwork contributes to achieving a consistent and polished final result.
