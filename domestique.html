ABOUT THE BRAND

Domestique is a furniture brand focused on timeless design, craftsmanship, and comfort. Each piece is created to tell a story and bring warmth, balance, and personality into any space.

The collection blends modern minimalism with classic elegance, offering furniture that feels both refined and inviting. Inspired by everyday living and the beauty of simplicity, Domestique seeks to create interiors that reflect authenticity and emotional connection.

Through attention to materials, form, and detail, every design is made to elevate the atmosphere of a home while remaining functional and enduring.

WORK PROCESS

01 ANALYSIS

02 PROTOTYPE

03 DESIGN

04 LAYOUT

Content and interface elements are arranged to ensure clear structure and usability.
